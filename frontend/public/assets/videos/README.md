# Video Assets Directory

Add your video files here:
- `1.mp4` - AI-generated video (sample)
- `2.mp4` - Real video (sample)
- `3.mp4` - AI-generated video (sample)
- `4.mp4` - Real video (sample)

## How to Use

1. Place your MP4 video files in this directory with names: `1.mp4`, `2.mp4`, `3.mp4`, `4.mp4`
2. The Deepfake Detection Simulation will automatically load and cycle through each video

## Video Specifications

- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 video codec recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical/portrait)
- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds recommended
- **File Size:** Keep under 5MB per video for optimal performance

## Notes

- Videos are muted and looped in the application
- The app will autoplay the video when it comes into view
- Users can scroll up/down to view different videos
- A question overlay appears asking users to identify if the video is AI-generated or real
